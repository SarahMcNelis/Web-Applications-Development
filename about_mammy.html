About Mammy

This website has been designed to show appreciation to all the Mammy's in the world, especially, my Mammy - Peggy.

This website showcases the incredibly talented woman my Mammy is. You can find pictures of all her delicious homemade treats under the Gallery Tab. And, if you think you might want to try some of Mammy's homemade goods, why not hit the Receipe Tab. All you need to do is login to access her secret recipes.

You never know, you might bake something to make someone else's life that little bit brighter!

Everyone needs a Mammy

Mammy's come in all shapes, sizes and forms. Some can be big sisters, small sisters, a cousin, an aunt, a neighbour, a friend, a Godmother, a Gran/Granny/Grandmother/Grandma/Nana/Nan/Nanny or maybe even a kind stranger.
One thing that is true in life is, we all need a Mammy.

Need a bit of comfort?

We all go through rocky times in life. We all have that bad day. We all need a little cry or rant. And sometimes we get overwhelmed. But the one thing to help make the world a little brighter is the sweet, glorious smell of Mammy's baking coming from the Kitchen.

Waiting for the call?

After that divine smell of baking comes a call down the hall from Mammy...

"Sarah.... Tea's ready"

And that's when you know it's time for a break. You walk into the kitchen and there looking back at you is Mammy sitting at the table, tea poured, and a slice of whatever delicious treat she's after baking waiting for you.
And with that break comes the councelling session. Free of charge!

Mammy's Gospel

Mammy doesn't always have to say anything. Sometimes, she's just the best listener. Sure who doesn't just need someone to just sit there and listen to you? Which is great when your mid way through a rant and just need to get it all off your chest.

But then sometimes Mammy will offer a few words of wisdom.
My Mammy likes to plant a seed of advise, almost like a clip out of a movie, gets up off the chair and walks away pretending to clean the counter or wash some dishes but really she's just giving me time to mull over the sensible suggestion she's just given me.

Smart woman my Mammy... maybe I should bake her a treat!
